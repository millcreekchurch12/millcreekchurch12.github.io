The Heart of Everything

Jesus Christ

The foundation of everything we believe, preach, and live at Millcreek Church.

Who Is Jesus?

The Son of God. The Savior of the World.

Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God — fully God and fully man. He was conceived of the Holy Spirit, born of the virgin Mary, lived a sinless life, and willingly gave Himself to die on the cross as the perfect sacrifice for our sin.

He rose bodily from the dead on the third day, ascended to the right hand of the Father, and will one day return in glory to judge the living and the dead. He is the one Mediator between God and man — and there is no salvation apart from faith in Him.

"For God so loved the world, that He gave His only Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have eternal life."

— John 3:16

How to Know Jesus

01

Recognize your need

All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God. We all need a Savior. (Romans 3:23)

02

Believe in Jesus

Confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead. (Romans 10:9)

03

Receive new life

Anyone who is in Christ is a new creation — the old has gone, the new has come. (2 Corinthians 5:17)
