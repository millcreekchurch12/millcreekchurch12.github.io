The Pathway

Seven Steps to Spiritual Growth

These seven steps guide every member of Millcreek Church — from the moment of salvation to multiplying disciples who make disciples.

01

Have a Personal Relationship with Jesus

Repent, believe, and receive. Begin a personal relationship with Jesus Christ through faith in His death and resurrection.

John 3:3; Romans 10:9–10
02

Be Baptized

Publicly identify as a disciple of Jesus Christ through believer's baptism by immersion.

Matthew 28:19–20; Romans 6:3–5
03

Become a Member & Join a Group

Join a Life Group, Discipleship Group, or Bible Study to grow in community.

Hebrews 10:24–25; Ephesians 5:25–30
04

Grow in Christlikeness

Worship, pray, read the Bible, give, fellowship, and share the Gospel daily.

2 Peter 3:18; Psalm 119:11
05

Be Equipped to Serve

Discover and develop your spiritual gifts for ministry in the body of Christ.

Romans 12:4–5; 1 Corinthians 12:27–28
06

Serve God and Others

Actively search for ways to serve the church and minister to the lost in Millcreek.

Mark 10:42–45; 1 Peter 4:10–11
07

Replicate in Others

Make disciples who make disciples — fulfilling the Great Commission together.

Matthew 28:19–20; 2 Timothy 2:2

Scripture

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit."
— Matthew 28:19

Ready to take your next step? Contact us and we will help connect you.
