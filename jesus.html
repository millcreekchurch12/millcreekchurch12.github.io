Why Give Online?

Giving at Millcreek Church

Millcreek Church offers you the ability to securely give online. You can select a one-time gift or conveniently set up a recurring gift to support the ministry of the Gospel in Millcreek, Utah.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." — 2 Corinthians 9:7

Help your church

Through online giving, you expedite your gift, simplify our accounting, and strengthen our base of regular support for ministry.

Increase your faithfulness

Online giving provides a way to strengthen your faithfulness and ensure your giving is consistent and intentional.

Prioritize giving

The Bible indicates giving is to be a high priority. Online, your giving is done automatically and consistently.

Encourage others

By being a forerunner in planned giving, you lead the way for others to grow in spiritual stewardship.

Other Ways to Give

By Mail — Check payable to Millcreek Church, 1515 E 4500 S, Millcreek, UT 84124

Zelle — Send to church@millcreekchurchutah.com (zero processing fees)
Planned Giving — Contact (801) 278-5683 for stock gifts, IRA distributions, or estate giving
