About Us

Our Vision & Mission

We believe God is calling us to be a catalyst for spiritual awakening in Millcreek and beyond.

Our Vision

A catalyst for spiritual awakening in Millcreek.

In Acts 1:8, Jesus said, "You will be My witnesses both in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and even to the remotest parts of the earth." That begins right here in Millcreek, Utah.

We are a House of Prayer — convinced that God works powerfully through small, faithful gatherings of believers who are willing to labor in the harvest.

Mission Statement

Love God. Love People. Share Jesus. Make Disciples.

From Matthew 22:38–39 and Matthew 28:19–20.

Meet Our Pastor

What We Value

Our Five Core Values

Intimacy With Jesus

Pursuing a passionate relationship with Jesus above all — in prayer, worship, and daily life.

Biblical Truth

Treasuring God's perfect, inerrant Word as our supreme standard for life, faith, and practice.

Intentional Hospitality

Welcoming every person — regardless of background — with the unconditional love of Jesus.

Ministry Excellence

Reflecting the character of God in service to others — doing everything as unto the Lord.

City Renewal

Investing in the redemption of the people of Millcreek — one life, one family at a time.

The Pathway

How We Make Disciples

01

Meet Jesus

Repent, believe, and receive a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. (John 3:3)

02

Be Baptized

Publicly identify as a disciple of Jesus Christ through believer's baptism. (Matthew 28:19–20)

03

Join the Church

Become a Millcreek member and join a Bible Study or Life Group. (Hebrews 10:24–25)

04

Grow Daily

Worship, pray, study Scripture, give, fellowship, and share the Gospel. (2 Peter 3:18)

05

Discover Your Gifts

Find and develop your spiritual gifts for service in the body of Christ. (Romans 12:4–5)

06

Serve

Actively serve the church and minister to the lost in Millcreek. (Mark 10:42–45)

07

Make Disciples

Replicate in others — make disciples who make disciples. (Matthew 28:19–20)

Ready to take your next step? We would love to help you on your journey.
