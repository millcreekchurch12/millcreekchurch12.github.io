Millcreek Men

At Millcreek Church, our Millcreek Men ministry exists to help every person grow in faith, connect in community, and serve our neighbors in Millcreek, Utah. We are rooted in Scripture, committed to prayer, and passionate about the Gospel.

"Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, as faithful stewards of God's grace in its various forms."

— 1 Peter 4:10
