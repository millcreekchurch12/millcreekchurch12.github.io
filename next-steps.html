About Us

We're glad you're here.

Since our beginning, Millcreek Church has been committed to ministering to our community and beyond. We believe God has placed us in Millcreek, Utah for a reason.

What to Expect

What are Sundays like?

Each service includes worship through song, prayer, baptism celebrations, and a biblical sermon from Pastor Dan Mamatela.

Worship & Praise

A blend of contemporary and traditional hymns delivered with passionate, reverent devotion to God.

Biblical Teaching

Pastor Dan delivers a message filled with biblical truth — practical, passionate, and rooted in Scripture.

Community & Prayer

We are a House of Prayer — every gathering includes prayer, fellowship, and celebration of God's work.
