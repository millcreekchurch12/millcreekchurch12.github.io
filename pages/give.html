Give to Millcreek Church

Your generosity pours the Gospel into Millcreek and beyond.

Why Give?

Your Gift Flows Far.

As living water reaches places we cannot see, your giving flows into outreach, discipleship, children's ministry, and the proclamation of the Gospel in Millcreek, Utah.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

— 2 Corinthians 9:7

Help your church

Online giving expedites your gift, simplifies accounting, and strengthens our regular support base.

Increase your faithfulness

Online giving ensures consistency and helps you prioritize giving in your budget — just as the tide is faithful.

Prioritize giving

The Bible calls giving a high priority — not a leftover matter. Set it up automatically and give with intention.

Encourage others

Your faithful giving sets an example that ripples outward — inspiring others to grow in generosity.
