Accessibility

We're Here for Everyone

Guest Parking

First-time guests are welcome to use our designated guest parking spaces near the main entrance.

Wheelchair Access

Our facility is fully wheelchair accessible. Please contact us for specific accommodation needs.

Spanish Translation

Spanish translation is available during Sunday worship. Ask an usher for a radio receiver upon arrival.

ASL Interpretation

American Sign Language interpretation is available. Reserved seating is provided for deaf and hard-of-hearing guests.

Nursery & Kids

Children's care is provided for ages birth through 3rd grade during Sunday worship services.

Contact Us

Have specific needs or questions? Please call (801) 278-5683 and we will be glad to help.
